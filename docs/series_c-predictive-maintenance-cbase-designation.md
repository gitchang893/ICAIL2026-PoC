# Series C Cbase Designation Notice
## Predictive Maintenance (Internal Component-Failure Prediction)

This Series C Cbase Designation Notice (this “Series C Notice”) is adopted as an Exhibit to, and forms part of, the LLC Agreement and expressly incorporates the **Common Cbase AI Governance Rider**. Accordingly, the governance structure applicable to Series C shall be **Cbase only**.

This Series C Notice applies solely with respect to **Series C (Predictive Maintenance)** (“Series C”), which is designated by the Company as the series responsible for **internal ML-based prediction of component failure using sensor data**.

In the event of any inconsistency between the Cbase Rider and this Series C Notice, this Series C Notice shall control solely with respect to Series C and solely to the extent necessary to identify the approved minimal-risk use case of Series C.

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Company acknowledges that internal predictive maintenance systems may improve maintenance planning and operational efficiency while ordinarily involving less external governance burden than limited-risk or high-risk AI uses;

WHEREAS, the Company has classified Series C as a **minimal-risk tier** use case and intends that the baseline governance clauses of Cbase shall apply without additional Chigh or Ctransparency supplements;

NOW, THEREFORE, the Company hereby adopts this Series C Notice as follows:

---

## ARTICLE I  
## SERIES C USE CASE; MINIMAL-RISK CLASSIFICATION

### Section 1.1 Approved Use Case

The Approved Use Case Boundary for Series C shall consist solely of internal predictive maintenance, failure forecasting, maintenance planning support, and related internal operational functions using sensor data.

### Section 1.2 Minimal-Risk Classification

Series C is designated as a **minimal-risk tier** AI series.

### Section 1.3 Required Personnel

For purposes of Cbase, the Company shall designate for Series C:

(a) a Maintenance Systems Manager; and  
(b) a Compliance or Operations Liaison.

---

## ARTICLE II  
## USE CASE LIMITATION

### Section 2.1 No External or Higher-Risk Drift

Series C shall not be used for customer-facing, rights-affecting, eligibility-related, or otherwise higher-risk functions without formal reclassification and reassignment to the governance structure designated by the Company.

### Section 2.2 No Supplemental Tier by Implication

Nothing in this Series C Notice shall be construed to adopt Chigh, Ctransparency, or any other supplemental clause set absent a separate written add-on adopted by the Company.

---

## ARTICLE III  
## BREACH; REMEDIES

### Section 3.1 Material Breach

Any knowing operation outside the Approved Use Case Boundary, knowing concealment of a Material Incident, or knowing use of Series C for a higher-risk function without reclassification shall constitute a material breach with respect to Series C.

### Section 3.2 Remedies

The Company may suspend deployment, narrow system use, require remediation, require additional review, or transfer the use case to another governance structure.
